Dimension 6 — Recovery / Living Environment
The client's home, social network, and community context and their impact on recovery.
⚠ A supportive environment is a step-down indicator for Dimension 6. If the client's environment is genuinely supportive, document in Additional Environmental Observations what environmental factors — if any — still contribute to the need for this LOC, and ensure other dimensions are carrying the clinical weight.
⚠ Stable housing is a step-down indicator. If the client's living situation is not a clinical barrier, ensure other dimensions justify the LOC and document in Additional Environmental Observations whether any housing-related factors still warrant clinical attention.
⚠ A strong support system is a step-down indicator. Document in Additional Environmental Observations how the client is utilizing this support and what — despite it — still requires the current level of care. Reviewers will question authorization when the environment is fully supportive.

      Changelog
      Clinical and workflow changes to DocAssist — most recent first.
      v1.4 June 16, 2026 Built-in training and tool guides
      Platform Full walkthrough tutorial added, accessible from the home screen. Covers all five tools with step-by-step spotlight guidance — ASAM documentation, ITP, individual and group notes, and discharge summary. A sample client loads automatically so every field is populated during training.
      Platform "Walkthrough" button added to each tool's header. Runs a focused walkthrough of just that tool without requiring the full training sequence. Useful for counselors who need a refresher on one specific tool.
      v1.3 June 11, 2026 Clinical flags, consultation prompts, discharge refinements
      ASAM Discharge added as a third document type alongside Initial Admission and Continued Stay. Each of the six ASAM dimensions now has discharge-specific fields capturing clinical status at the time of discharge, generating a narrative structured for discharge documentation rather than continued stay justification.
      ASAM Severe Dimension 3 concerns now trigger a consultation flag, prompting supervisor review to confirm the client is appropriate for the current level of care before documentation is finalized.
      Group Notes Non-participation checkbox added for individual clients. When checked, the note reflects poor prognosis and includes a prompt to follow up personally with the client after group.
      Discharge Days in treatment now calculates automatically from admission date and date of last contact. Resource sheet documentation added — records whether the client received a sheet at discharge, and requires an explanation if not.
      v1.2 June 10, 2026 ITP enhancements; ASAM LOC-conditional fields
      ITP Section C now includes standard Case Management objectives. Select which apply via a checkbox panel; a master toggle collapses the entire CM block for clients with these already established in Remarkable.
      ITP Justification for Transition added for five LOC transitions: 3.7WM→3.5, 3.7→3.5, 3.5→2.5, 2.5→2.1, and 2.1→1.0. Supports documenting skipped levels when a client declines the recommended next LOC.
      ASAM CIWA-Ar, COWS, and withdrawal symptom fields now only appear when 3.7WM is selected. They are hidden for all other levels of care.
      v1.1 June 9, 2026 Group Notes and Discharge Summary launched
      Group Notes Group Notes tool launched. Set up a session once and generate individual notes for each client. Includes prognosis selection (Poor/Fair/Good), a shared intervention block copied once for all clients, and billing warnings for Good prognosis and ratios over 12:1.
      Discharge Discharge Summary tool launched. Generates the free-text fields in Remarkable not auto-populated from the ITP, including progress summary, continuity of care, and aftercare plan. Includes a phase history builder with auto-calculated Dosage Hours for Sublocade/Vivitrol documentation.
      v1.0 June 8, 2026 Initial launch Launch
      ASAM ASAM Documentation tool with six dimension tabs, severity selectors, LOC and document type selection, required field validation, and two output tabs: Narrative and Criteria & Risk Ratings.
      ITP ITP Development tool with 29 goal categories across Sections A, B, and C, preset goal templates with SMART warnings, and opt-in objectives and interventions.
      Ind. Notes Individual Session Notes tool with two output blocks matching Remarkable's note fields. Supports four session types: Working off ITP, Updating ITP, First Meeting, and Crisis Intervention.
